EverAsk successfully equipped with international brand protection

27 April 2023

Now that EverAsk was already protected for Switzerland, Meeting Kitchen was able to successfully extend the trademark protection. The EverAsk trademark has also been registered as an international trademark (IR trademark) since April.

EverAsk is a digital assistant that supports patients in clarifying open questions and addressing uncertainties in medical consultations.

EverAsk can be used as a website or native application.

It works for medical practices and hospitals. Depending on the application scenario, EverAsk can be specifically adapted to the needs of the patients on the one hand and to those of the hospital on the other.
